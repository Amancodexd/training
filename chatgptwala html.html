About the Movie

Spider-Man: Far From Home is a 2019 Marvel superhero film that follows Peter Parker after the events of Avengers: Endgame. During a school trip to Europe, Peter hopes to enjoy a normal vacation. However, Nick Fury recruits him to stop the dangerous Elementals. Peter joins forces with Mysterio, but later discovers that Mysterio is actually the villain behind the attacks.
